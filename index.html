  • Middle School Wins Football Game Over Naserath

    The Middle School football team completed it’s year with a 28-18 win over Nazareth. For the second straight year the Hornets went undefeated (3-0-2) and are League Champions! Reilly Bechtel led the team with 3 rushing TD’s and over 200 yards on the ground. Rafy Torerro returned a kickoff for a TD, too. The defense was led by Trystan Fulmer and Connor Soos. In the B Game, the Hornets won again as Jerek Cooper and Michael Milott both found the end zone with rushing TD’s in a 16-8 victory.
